Private SaaS (PSaaS) Appliance

An Auth0 deployment that exists in a dedicated area of Auth0's cloud, your cloud, or your own data center.

PSaaS Appliance Planning

PSaaS Appliance is a managed service that is typically used if your organization's compliance or policy requirements prevent you from using a multi-tenant cloud service.

  • Overview

    The PSaaS Appliance is an option for your organization when compliance or other policy requirements prevent you from using a multi-tenant cloud service.

  • Customer-Hosted PSaaS Appliance

    For customers with PSaaS Appliance hosted in a dedicated area of their own cloud or data center.

  • Auth0-Hosted PSaaS Appliance

    For customers with PSaaS Appliance hosted using Auth0's Dedicated (Private) Cloud Service.
